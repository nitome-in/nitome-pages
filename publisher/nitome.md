# Nitome

## Overview

**Nitome** is an innovative EdTech aggregation platform designed to make quality exam preparation more accessible and affordable for students. It brings together educational content from multiple sources and connects educators with learners through a collaborative digital ecosystem.

The platform aims to simplify the learning journey by providing students with a centralized destination for discovering, accessing, and utilizing high-quality study resources.

---

## Mission

To democratize education by providing affordable access to quality exam preparation content while empowering educators to reach a wider audience.

---

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Content Aggregation | Access study materials from multiple educators and content providers in one platform |
| Affordable Learning | Cost-effective alternatives to traditional coaching and exam preparation programs |
| Educator Network | Connects teachers, mentors, and subject experts with students |
| Centralized Access | Single platform for discovering and consuming educational content |
| Collaborative Ecosystem | Encourages knowledge sharing between educators and learners |
| Exam-Focused Preparation | Curated resources tailored for academic and competitive examinations |

|------------|------------|
