Menaka Publication, established in the 1970s, is a renowned publishing house based in Pune, Maharashtra. It is widely known for its contributions to Marathi literature, education, and journalism. The publication is known for its magazines, educational books, and literary works that cater to a Marathi-speaking audience.

Popular Magazines

  • Menaka: A leading Marathi magazine for women, covering health, beauty, fashion, cooking, family life, and more.
  • Lokprabha: A weekly news magazine focusing on current affairs, politics, and social issues in Maharashtra and India.
  • Balmitra: A children's magazine featuring stories, puzzles, and educational content designed to engage young readers.

Educational Books and Guides

  • Scholarship Exam Guides: Menaka Publication is trusted for its guides and preparation material for Maharashtra's 5th and 8th-grade scholarship exams, helping students excel in these competitive tests.
  • Other Educational Material: They also publish workbooks, practice sheets, and preparation guides for various competitive exams.

Marathi Literature and Fiction

Menaka Publication is actively involved in promoting Marathi literature. They publish Marathi novels, short stories, biographies, and self-help books that resonate with readers across Maharashtra. Their works support local authors and encourage the spread of Marathi culture.

Focus on Marathi Culture and Language

The publication is dedicated to fostering Marathi culture through its content. It emphasizes topics relevant to the regional audience and is instrumental in preserving the language's rich literary tradition.

Digital Presence

In addition to print media, Menaka Publication has adapted to the digital age by offering online versions of its magazines and books, expanding its reach to a global audience.
