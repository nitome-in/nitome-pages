# Examination Pattern

The examination places:

- **Less emphasis on knowledge-based questions**
- **More emphasis on:**
    - Understanding
    - Reasoning
    - Correlation
    - Application of concepts
    - Scientific thinking
    - Observation and analysis

### Question Sources

- Majority of questions are based on **Maharashtra State Board Science textbooks**
- Relevant content from **CBSE, ICSE, and IB Science textbooks**
- General knowledge in science
- Science in everyday life

# Key Success Tips

✅ Understand concepts instead of memorizing facts
✅ Relate scientific concepts to everyday life
✅ Practice reasoning and application-based questions
✅ Revise textbook diagrams, activities, and experiments
✅ Stay curious about science and current developments
✅ Solve previous HBYS question papers and mock tests whenever available
