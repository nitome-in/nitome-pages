
**Pre-Upper Primary Scholarship Examination** is an important examination conducted by the Maharashtra State Council of Examination, Pune. This exam is meant for intelligent and meritorious students studying in Class 5, with the aim of providing them financial assistance for further education. The examination includes language skills, mathematical concepts, and an intelligence test. Complete details regarding the exam, application process, syllabus, and results are available on the official website (https://2025.puppssmsce.in/). Students or schools can easily log in to the portal and complete all the required procedures.

The syllabus for the Pre-Upper Primary Scholarship Examination (Class 5) is determined by the Maharashtra State Council of Examination, Pune, as per the government’s decision.

This examination consists of two papers in total:

**Paper 1:**
This paper includes First Language i.e. English and Mathematics. Focus is given to linguistic skills such as grammar, reading comprehension, and writing. The Mathematics syllabus covers numbers, mathematical operations, geometry, statistics, and practical mathematics.

**Paper 2:**
This paper includes Third Language i.e Marathi and an Intelligence Test. The intelligence test evaluates logical reasoning, problem-solving ability, and mental flexibility.
