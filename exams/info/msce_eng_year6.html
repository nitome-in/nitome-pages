Exam Structure

Number of Papers: Two

Total Marks: 300 marks (150 marks for each paper)

Time Duration: 1 hour 30 minutes (90 minutes) per paper

Number of Questions: 75 questions per paper

Type of Questions: Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)

Paper Pattern

Paper 1

  • First Language (Marathi/Urdu/Hindi/English): 25 Questions (50 Marks)
  • Mathematics: 50 Questions (100 Marks)

Paper 2

  • Second Language (Marathi/Urdu/Hindi/English): 25 Questions (50 Marks)
  • Intelligence Test (Mental Ability): 50 Questions (100 Marks)

Marking Scheme

  • Each correct answer: 2 marks
  • No negative marking for wrong answers

Subjects Covered

First and Second Language: Questions focus on grammar, vocabulary, comprehension, and usage.

Mathematics: Includes arithmetic, geometry, and basic problem-solving.

Intelligence Test: Covers logical reasoning, pattern identification, analogies, and series completion.
