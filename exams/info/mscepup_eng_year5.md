**Former Upper Primary Scholarship Examination** is an important competitive exam conducted by the Maharashtra State Council of Examination, Pune. The objective is to award scholarships by evaluating the academic quality and intelligence of Class 5 students.

### Exam Structure:
- **Paper 1:** English and Mathematics – 150 marks
- **Paper 2:** Marathi and Intelligence – 150 marks
- **Question Type:** Objective (MCQ) with four options
- **Eligibility:** Minimum 40% marks required in each paper

| Paper       | Subject     | No. of Questions | Marks | Time |
|------------|-------------|-----------------|------|------|
| **Paper 1** | English     | 25 | 50 | 1 hour 30 minutes |
|            | Mathematics | 50 | 100 |

| Paper       | Subject           | No. of Questions | Marks | Time |
|------------|-------------------|-----------------|------|------|
| **Paper 2** | Marathi           | 25 | 50 | 1 hour 30 minutes |
|            | Intelligence Test | 50 | 100 |

### Question Difficulty Level:

| Difficulty Level         | Percentage |
|--------------------------|------------|
| Easy questions           | 30%        |
| Medium-level questions   | 40%        |
| Difficult questions      | 30%        |
